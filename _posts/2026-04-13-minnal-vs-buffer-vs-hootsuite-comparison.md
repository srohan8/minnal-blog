## The Deep Dive

### Buffer: The Simple Scheduler

**What it does well:**
- Clean, intuitive interface
- Reliable posting across major platforms
- Basic analytics
- Affordable for individuals ($6-12/month)
- Queue system for consistent posting

**What it lacks:**
- No AI visibility tracking
- Limited content strategy guidance
- No integration with knowledge sources (Notion, etc.)
- You still need to write all content manually
- Analytics are surface-level

**Best for:** Solo creators who just need simple, reliable scheduling and already know what to post.

**Price:** Starting at $6/month (Essentials), $12/month (Team), $120/month (Agency)

### Hootsuite: The Enterprise Tool

**What it does well:**
- Massive platform coverage (20+ networks)
- Advanced team collaboration
- Social listening and monitoring
- Detailed analytics and reporting
- Bulk scheduling

**What it lacks:**
- Expensive for solo founders ($99+/month)
- Complex interface with steep learning curve
- No LLM visibility tracking
- Overkill for most indie hackers
- AI features are surface-level

**Best for:** Marketing teams managing multiple brands with dedicated social media managers.

**Price:** Starting at $99/month (Professional), $249/month (Team), Custom (Enterprise)

### Minnal: The AI-Native Platform

**What it does well:**
- **LLM Visibility Tracking:** Monitors ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, Grok
- **Content Strategy AI:** Generates week-long plans based on your docs
- **Conversation Interface:** Works inside Claude Desktop via MCP
- **GEO Optimization:** Suggests tactics to improve AI visibility
- **Knowledge Integration:** Pulls from Notion, Google Drive, GitHub
- **Content Gap Analysis:** Shows what competitors cover that you don't
- **Learning Loop:** Analyzes what works and iterates

**What it lacks:**
- Newer platform (less established)
- Smaller platform coverage (growing)
- Requires Claude Desktop for best experience

**Best for:** Solo founders who want AI to handle marketing strategy, not just scheduling.

**Price:** Free tier available, Pro at $29/month

## The Real Difference: Scheduling vs Strategy

**Buffer and Hootsuite solve:** "When should I post this?"

**Minnal solves:** "What should my marketing strategy be?"

The workflow difference:

**Traditional tools (Buffer/Hootsuite):**
1. You write content manually
2. You decide when to post
3. Tool schedules it
4. Tool shows basic metrics
5. You interpret metrics yourself
6. Repeat

**AI-native (Minnal):**
1. You tell Claude your goals
2. AI generates full strategy
3. AI writes content (GEO-optimized)
4. AI schedules optimally
5. AI analyzes performance
6. AI iterates next week
7. You approve/tweak
